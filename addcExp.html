Water Connection Engineer

Summary of Job

• Preparation of detailed designs for various Water Infrastructure Projects.
• Preparation of technical reports and bill of quantities.
• Reviewing material submittals and shop drawings.
• Liaise with Municipality for route approvals. (Through CDP)
• Preparation of construction documents.
• Conducting Site Surveys, Designing and planning for water network enhancement projects.
• Issuing / Planning work orders and material requests through Maximo.
• Construction Supervision.
• Conducting inspections on Water Supply installations.
• Preparation of Pre-defined inspection remarks and monitor for continual improvement.
• Leading a team of design engineers by providing them with technical solutions and decisions which are complying with ADDC/ADWEA/RSB regulations.
• Preparing the changes required in the process / system in order to comply with the company’s mission.
• Preparing the KPI related reports.
• Conducting trainings on different areas for the design team and inspection team.
• Authorizing water connections.
